To my beloved

Hello rin, happy Valentine's Day! I am very happy that this is our first Valentine's Day together, and I would like to take this opportunity to write my first letter to you.

There is a Chinese saying that goes, "Love is separated by mountains and seas, and mountains and seas can be level.". We are truly long distance couples, but the love in our hearts can erase the influence of distance and time on us. I prefer to believe that our relationship has never been uncertain, but has developed step by step to the present steadfast love. The two of us have made promises for the rest of our lives.

My longing for you is increasing day by day. I wish I could grow wings and fly to your side, never to be separated from you again. But I also know that we are still in the stage of studying, and although love is precious, pursuing academic success is still one of our main themes at this stage. I also believe that we can achieve the happiness we desire in both love and studies by playing harmoniously.

I love you dear, too many sweet words cannot express my love for you. Dear Rin, let's hold hands together, heart to heart, and rush towards a better tomorrow!

yours huayu
